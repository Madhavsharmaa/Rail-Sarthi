Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book train tickets online?

You can book train tickets online through various travel booking websites and apps. Ensure you have a valid ID and payment method.

What documents are required for train travel?

Valid ID proof such as Aadhar card, voter ID, passport, or driving license is required for train travel.

How can I check the train schedule?

You can check the train schedule through travel booking websites, apps, or at the railway station inquiry counter.

What is the baggage allowance for train travel?

Generally, passengers are allowed to carry up to 40kg of luggage in sleeper class and up to 70kg in AC class. However, it's best to check specific guidelines.

Are there any discounts available for senior citizens?

Yes, senior citizens are eligible for discounts on train tickets. The discount percentage and eligibility criteria can be checked on travel booking websites.
